Lablr

Tag your images

Lablr is label/tag recommendation tool for flickr images allowing the users to use the most relevant labels for their images to maximize the viewship of their photos. Lablr suggests the most relevant label from the all time most popular tags used on flickr.

Text annonymizer

Automatically annomize documents

Text Anonymizer is a text anonymization app for journalists, newscasters, and content producers. It uses natural language processing to enable the users to anonymize different parts of a text (e.g., name of individuals, locations, dates, etc.). The app provides the option to replace the names (e.g., individuals, countries, organizations, etc.) with place holders or with fake names.

fMRI quality assurance (QA)

Quality assurance for a longitudinal multi-site fMRI study accross Canada

Analysis conducted in collaboration with the Ontario Neurodegeneration Disease Research Initiative (ONDRI) and the Canadian Biomarker Integration Network in Depression (CAN-BIND). The aim was to ensure quality data are collected at different sites and over time. The study involved 13 sites accross Canada and ran for more than 3 years.
